Our "Why"

SockOps is a small, practical platform to stop single-sock loss. We offer a searchable register for lost-and-found socks where users can see the latest locations of found socks using a hover function to see pinned sightings of them on a state level map. Our service coordinates trained community response teams--including formally organized “deploy a mom” search-and-recovery squads--and links to step-by-step repair. We prioritize measurable, repair-first solutions and each listing includes tried-and-true tips for locating the missing half of your pair.
